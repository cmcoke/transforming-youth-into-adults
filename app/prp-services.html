PRP SERVICES

Psychiatric Rehabilitation is a set of holistic services we offer to assist and support individuals who suffer from debilitating mental health conditions. PRP services are an extension to therapy services and reinforce the psycho-therapeutic aspects of an individual's mental health treatment by addressing what happens internally, mentally, and emotionally.We make use of personalized collaborative strategies to help with recovery, integration into society, and improvements to personal well-being and quality of life, with the end goal of empowering participants to increase their levels of success and satisfaction in the roles and environments of their choice.

Using a strength-based approach, participants are invited to explore the roles that are important to them in the key areas of living, learning, working, and socializing, and to develop the ability to make significant choices and create opportunities in these areas.

  • Our staff will assist clients by using interventions to promote the use of community resources and self-help organizations to support the individual to participate fully in the community of their choice.

  • All services are offered at times and places, according to the needs of the individuals served and coordinated with other mental health services including the referring therapist.

  • Get access to high level professional support and potential therapies under expert supervision

  • Get treatment that addresses every aspect of your mental well-being

Intake and Referral

How to receive prp services:

PRP services are billable through medical assistance. To be eligible to receive psychiatric rehabilitation services in the state of Maryland:

You must have a mental health diagnosis included in the Priority Population as defined by the Maryland Department of Health & Mental Hygiene.

Your impairment(s) can be expected to be stabilized at this level of care.

The impairment results in at least one of the following:

  • A clear, current threat to the Individual's ability to live in their customary setting

  • An inability to be employed or attend school without support

  • An inability to manage the effects of the Individual's mental illness

  • Your condition requires an integrated program of rehabilitation services to develop and restore independent living skills to support your recovery.

All clients must be engaged in outpatient mental health treatment (therapy) for at least 45 days to receive services.

All less intensive levels of treatment have been determined to be unsafe or unsuccessful.

PRP ELIGIBILITY

INTAKE FOR ADULTS

To be eligible for our program, adults must satisfy ALL of the following requirements:

  1. Referral from treating Licensed Mental Health Professional

  2. Maryland Medicaid and/or proof of application

  3. A psychiatric diagnosis that meets the priority population definition as determined by the Mental Health Authority

  4. Intake and Rehabilitation Assessment with staff

INTAKE FOR YOUTHS

To be eligible for our program, youth must satisfy ALL of the following requirements:

  1. 5-17 years of age

  2. Referral from treating Licensed Mental Health Professional

  3. Have an Axis I diagnosis from a licensed mental health professional

  4. Currently, have ACTIVE Maryland State Medicaid

  5. Intake and Rehabilitation Assessment with staff (Parent or Legal Guardian Must be Present)
