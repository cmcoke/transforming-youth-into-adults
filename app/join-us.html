Join our team!

The rehabilitation team is composed of an Executive Director, Licensed Rehabilitation Specialists and Rehabilitation Counselors. Our team ensures a consistent, safe and rehabilitative environment for each client, working with them to overcome anxiety, depression, trauma, anger, personality disorders, chronic mental illness, relationship struggles and behavior challenges. Staff are trained to assist clients with learning-age and developmentally appropriate skills to manage behavior. Our skilled practitioners use evidence-based services and techniques designed to assist in the development of insights and support.
